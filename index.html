About us

A home away from home, since 2011

Founded in 2011, Cambridge University Bangla Society welcomes members of Cambridge University from all backgrounds regardless of ethnicity, nationality, culture or faith.

We are an organisation that has created a community which celebrates and educates about the cultures of Bengal and Bangladesh at one of the world's top-ranked universities. We run social and networking events, share Bengali culture with the wider university, and support access and welfare for our members.

What we do

Community, Culture & Access

Three pillars that guide everything we do at BanglaSoc.

Social & Networking

We run events for our members so they can get to know each other, build friendships and professional links within and beyond the university.

Culture & Heritage

We share Bengali culture with the rest of the University through speaker events, cultural evenings, and celebrations of Bangladesh's rich heritage.

Access & Welfare

We provide support for prospective Cambridge applicants from Bangladesh and Bengali communities, and welfare support for our current members.
